# Chessnoth – Branding Board

## Brand Overview
**Chessnoth** is a tactical fantasy RPG inspired by chess-like strategy, turn-based combat, and a dark yet elegant fantasy universe.

**Core Keywords**: Strategy, Fantasy, Tactical, Arcane, Precision, Power

The brand should feel **intelligent, serious, and epic**, avoiding cartoonish or overly grim tones.

## Typography

### Display / Titles
Used for logo, headers, zones, bosses.

**Recommended fonts:**
- Cinzel
- Trajan Pro (if available)
- Uncial Antiqua

**Characteristics:**
- Serif
- Elegant
- High contrast
- Fantasy-inspired

### UI / Body Text
Used for menus, stats, descriptions.

**Recommended fonts:**
- Inter
- Manrope
- Source Sans 3

### Numeric / Technical (Optional)
Used for stats, blockchain info, debugging.

- JetBrains Mono
- Space Mono

## Logo Usage

### Variants
- Icon-only (sword + chessboard)
- Full logo (icon + wordmark)

### Guidelines
- Prefer dark backgrounds
- Subtle glow or light rim allowed
- Never distort or rotate
- Do not apply heavy shadows or gradients

---

## Visual Style

### User Interface
- Dark mode by default
- Soft rounded corners (8–12px)
- Subtle glassmorphism
- Minimal glow effects (blue or purple)

### Characters & Units
- Isometric perspective
- Clear silhouettes over fine detail
- Saturated but controlled colors
- Semi-realistic fantasy style

### Environments & Zones
- Strong biome identity
- Stylized, not realistic
- Consistent lighting language across zones

- Clean geometric shapes
